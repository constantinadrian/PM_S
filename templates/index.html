What is PM-Schedule?

It's a simple web application designed for Non-Profits & Businesses. PM-Schedule helps companies create, schedule and follow an effective preventive maintenance program. Two of the benefits for using a PM schedule include reducing equipment downtime and increasing asset its entire lifecycle.

What does it do?

PM-Schedule keep tracks of your preventive maintenance jobs. Once a PM is complete it will automatic generate an invoice for that job. Keeping records has never been so easy before.

How do you use it?

At a basic level, PM Schedule is simple: set a job that needs to be done, set the date and time, and assign someone to perform the work. When the time comes, the person that was assign carry out the job, and the scheduled event is either completed (once) or is repeated at a specified interval.
